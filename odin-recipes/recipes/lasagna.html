Lasagna

Description

Lasagne is a classic Italian al forno dish that generally consists of alternating layers of pasta sheets and various fillings, including - though not exclusively - rag√π, besciamella and cheese, such as mozzarella, ricotta or Parmesan. Typically, this assembled dish is then baked in an oven until piping hot and all the cheese has melted.

Beloved worldwide, lasagne is especially popular in the US, where they celebrate national Lasagne day on the 29th July.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of geound meat
  • One whole diced onion
  • 1 canned and 1 crushed tomattoes
  • Herbs: Parsley and one clove of Garlic
  • A dash of Sugar
  • Seasonings:
    • Dried basil
    • Dried oregano
    • Salt
    • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles/sheets
  • Cheese:
    • Cottage cheese
    • Parmesan
    • Shredded mozzarella
  • Eggs

Cooking steps:

  1. Cook the meat: Cook the ground meat in a skillet untill browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles/sheets in lightly salted water until the're al dente.
  3. Make the cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Assemble the lasgna: Layer the ingreidents according to the reipe (staring wth sauc3 and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
  5. Bake the lasagna: Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about haf an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown.
